I am a Ph.D. student in Sociology and an M.S. student in Data Science at Brown University (GPA 4.0/4.0). My work sits at the intersection of computational social science and machine learning, with projects on (1) how data production shapes algorithmic bias, (2) measuring LLM reasoning using large-scale social survey data, and (3) large-scale text analysis of climate discourse.

I previously worked as a Data Scientist Intern at Supio (LLM-based narrative generation pipelines; interactive Streamlit dashboard) and a Research Intern at Lifespan (3D medical imaging, 3D U-Net / TransNet).
